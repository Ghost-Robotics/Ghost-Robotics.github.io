Who Am I?

I was born in Sydney, Nova Scotia, but I have lived in Woodstock, New Brunswick for as long as I can remember. I've had a knack for writing and it is through communications that I channel this passion into high-quality work.

With a St. Thomas University education guiding my work, I am an eager communications professional and journalist who's always improving himself. This isn't just a line of work: its a passion for me, and I use that passion to create quality writing and an effective message through all kinds of media.
